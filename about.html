Project Overview

Mera Aas Padose is an arts-integrated initiative aimed at empowering students from BND Public School in South Delhi to explore, document, and celebrate their surroundings through various art forms. This project bridges identity gaps faced by immigrant students and fosters a sense of belonging by reconnecting them with their cultural heritage.

Significance of the Project

The project supports students from socially and economically disadvantaged backgrounds, particularly from migrant populations. By embedding art into education, it enhances creativity, academic engagement, and emotional expression.

Goals and Objectives

  • Cultural Reconnection: Students document their home and neighborhood environments to explore cultural identity.
  • Academic Integration: Language subjects (Hindi/English) are woven into interactive and creative activities.
  • Creative Expression: Students express ideas through drawing, drama, storytelling, and model making.
  • Community Engagement: The community is involved through exhibitions and storytelling events.

Expected Outcomes

  • Enhanced student self-expression and confidence
  • Greater cultural awareness within schools and communities
  • Improved language and academic performance
  • Stronger community relationships through collaborative engagement
